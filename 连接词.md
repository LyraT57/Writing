### 顺承与递进：顺着说，往上顶
（用于延续前一个观点，或使论述更深一层）

Firstly, Secondly, Finally... (第一，第二，最后...)

Furthermore, / Moreover, (此外，) - 比 and 更高级。

More importantly, (更重要的是，)

What is more, (而且，)

Not only... but also... (不仅...而且...)

Similarly, / Likewise, (类似地，)

作文实战：
"Regular exercise improves physical health. Furthermore, it significantly benefits mental well-being by reducing stress."

### 对比与转折：亮出另一面
（用于引出不同、相反或意想不到的观点，制造思辨性)

However, / Nevertheless, / Nonetheless, (然而，) - 作文三巨头，比 but 更正式。

On the contrary, / Conversely, (正相反，) - 强调直接对立。

Whereas... / While... (而...)

On the one hand... On the other hand... (一方面...另一方面...) - 经典对比结构。

Despite / In spite of (+ noun phrase) (尽管...)

作文实战：
"Technology has made our lives more convenient. However, over-reliance on it may lead to social isolation."

### 因果与推理：讲清来龙去脉
（用于解释原因、推导结果，让论证严密）

Therefore, / Thus, / Consequently, / As a result, (因此，) - 引出结果。

As, / Because, / Since (因为，) - 引出原因。

Due to / Owing to (+ noun phrase) (由于...)

So... that... / Such... that... (如此...以至于...)

作文实战：
"The demand for the product was incredibly high. Consequently, the price increased dramatically."

### 举例与强调：让观点更扎实
（用具体事例支撑观点，或加重语气）

For example, / For instance, (例如，)

Such as, / Like (比如，)

In particular, / Particularly, (特别是，)

Indeed, / As a matter of fact, (事实上，)

作文实战：
"Many great inventions were initially met with skepticism. For instance, the telephone was once considered a useless toy."

### 总结与结论：有力收尾
（用于段落或全文结尾，给出最终判断）

In conclusion, / To sum up, / In summary, (总之，)

In a word, / In brief, (简而言之，)

All in all, / Overall, (总的来说，)

From what has been discussed above, we can draw the conclusion that... (通过以上讨论，我们可以得出结论...)

作文实战：
"In conclusion, while challenges remain, the potential benefits of artificial intelligence are too significant to ignore."
